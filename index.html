About Sam Sage

Christian Fortuno, better known as Sam Sage, is a dynamic Singer-Songwriter and Producer from Puerto Rico, known for his unique blend of Reggaeton and Trap music that resonates in both English and Spanish. With over 10 years of experience in the industry, Sam has cultivated a signature style marked by melodic richness and a heartfelt, loving feel that runs through every track.

Born in Puerto Rico, Sam’s deep cultural roots and passion for music shine through his compositions, creating a sound that feels both authentic and innovative. He has had the opportunity to collaborate with notable artists such as Bryant Myers and Kendo Kaponi, further cementing his place within the Latin music scene and showcasing his ability to blend diverse influences into his work. As a composer for various artists and an audio engineer of his own vocals, he brings a multifaceted skill set to every project, ensuring that each production is polished, emotive, and distinctly his own.

Currently making waves on the Latin music scene in Austin (ATX), Sam Sage is the artist to watch as he continues to gain momentum. Check out his upcoming performance at SXSW 2025.
