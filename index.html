About Sam Sage

Christian Fortuno, better known as Sam Sage, is a dynamic Singer-Songwriter and Producer from Puerto Rico with a unique blend of Reggaeton and Trap.

Collaborations with artists like Bryant Myers and Kendo Kaponi have solidified his place in the Latin urban scene.

Currently making waves in Austin (ATX), Sam Sage is the artist to watch. Catch him live at SXSW 2025.
